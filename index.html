About New Jeans

New Jeans is a K-pop group that has won the hearts of fans from all over the world. Their music blends genres like synth-pop, hip hop, and R&B, with influences from the 1990s and 2000s.

New Jeans was formed under HYPE Entertainment under the label ADOR. Consisting of 5 members, the group debuted in 2022 with the song "Attention."

Some members have contributed in the writing process of their song releases.

Support New Jeans

The official fanclub name for New Jeans is called "Bunnies." The mascot of New Jeans is also a bunny.

In 2023, New Jeans came out with their official light stick, which is also in the shape of a white bunny. Their light stick has an offical name called "Binky Bong."

The light stick comes in 6 colors: white, green, pink, blue, orange, and yellow. The initial run also included changeable parts for personal customization.

To support New Jeans, consider streaming their music, and buying their albums either online or in stores.
